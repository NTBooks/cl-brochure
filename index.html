The problem

Digital records weren’t built for the speed of today

Organizations are under pressure to modernize, but the records they produce — credentials, contracts, AI-generated work — are easy to fake and slow to verify.

Easy to fake
AI tools can generate convincing diplomas, legal documents, and certificates in minutes — traditional PDFs offer no built-in protection
Slow to verify
Employers, courts, and counterparties wait days or weeks for organizations to manually confirm records via email or phone
Expensive to change
Legacy systems require long procurement cycles, IT projects, and months of implementation before a single record is stamped

Why blockchain

Records that can’t be faked. Even by AI

Stamping records on the blockchain solves every weakness of traditional digital files.

Tamper-proof

Immutable by design

Once a record is stamped on the blockchain, it cannot be altered, backdated, or deleted — by anyone, including Chainletter. The cryptographic hash makes forgery mathematically impossible.

Instant

Verify in seconds

Anyone with the file can verify its authenticity instantly — no phone calls, no emails, no waiting. Employers, courts, licensing boards, and AI agents get a definitive answer immediately.

Permanent

Outlasts any organization

Blockchain records exist independently of Chainletter or the issuing organization. Even if an entity closes, its stamped records remain verifiable forever on the public blockchain.
