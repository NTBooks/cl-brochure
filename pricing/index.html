frequently asked questions

Do you offer a free trial?

We can offer demos or trials on a case-by-case basis, please contact us if you are interested. We will not be able to host any files once a trial ends if a paid subscription is not activated.

What happens if I cancel?

Before cancelling your account you should make sure you have local copies of all your hosted files. Much like a web host, if you are not an active subscriber your hosted files will be deleted. All the stamps you have created exist on the blockchain forever, so local copies of your files can still be validated. If you ever resubscribe you can upload your files back to your account and everything will return to how it was before cancellation.

The institution’s verification pages will revert to generic Chainletter branding.

Do you support educational discount?

Our product is currently priced for the educational market and is not offered at further discount.

Do you have iPhone and Android apps?

Professional use of our software requires a full desktop experience is necessary to properly use the management and upload tools.

The verification tools are mobile friendly but we do not have an app at this time.

Is there any free support included?

Email based support is provided to non-enterprise customers and we will do our best to respond in a timely manner during normal business hours.

Can I integrate with 3rd party apps?

Yes! We have a full third party API and some demo applications. Links will be added here once they become available to customers.
